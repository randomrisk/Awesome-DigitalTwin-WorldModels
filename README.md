| 2026-06-25 | Generative Models on Analog Hardware with Dynamics | Yu-Neng Wang, Sara Achour | [Link](http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27294v1) | Analog hardware platforms such as coupled oscillators and Analog Ising Machines naturally solve differential equations at a fraction of the energy cost of digital computation, making them attractive for low-power generative modeling, yet a fundamental mismatch exists: modern generative models assume flexible, software-defined dynamics, whereas analog hardware imposes fixed, physics-determined differential equations with limited approximation capacity.
