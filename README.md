This study investigates how NF-induced stress redistribution governs HF-NF interactions through coupled laboratory experiments and poroelastic extended finite element simulations on intact and pre-fractured PMMA specimens under plane-strain conditions. Digital image correlation provides full-field measurements of displacement and strain evolution during mechanical loading and hydraulic fracturing.
